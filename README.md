# Hi, I'm Elvis Appiah 👋

I'm a Civil Engineer from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Ghana, with a First Class BSc and a research background spanning urban flood risk, sustainable drainage, nature-based solutions, and transportation engineering. I am actively pursuing postgraduate opportunities in water resources and transportation engineering.

I use this GitHub to document my engineering projects — from hydrological modelling and flood analysis to pavement design and transportation infrastructure. My goal is to build research capacity in West Africa and contribute to infrastructure policy through rigorous, data-driven engineering.
